Department of Computer Science & Engineering

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering focuses on providing strong theoretical foundations and practical skills to prepare students for the rapidly evolving field of information technology. The department emphasizes innovation, problem-solving, and hands-on learning.

Students are encouraged to engage in research, industry projects, and skill-based learning to meet global standards. With experienced faculty, modern laboratories, and a learner-centered approach, the department aims to produce competent professionals and future leaders.
